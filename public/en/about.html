    Who Are We?

    Irit Filipovich – Senior Archivist and Founder of Family PastPort

    As an archivist, I have dedicated most of my professional life to the craft of documentation. Having led the Archives Division at Yad Vashem and managed leading companies in the fields of archiving and information management, I learned that behind every piece of information, document, or photograph, lies an entire world of emotion, identity, and human history.
    For me, an archive is not merely a collection of papers, but a living mosaic of human destinies and a living bridge between the past and the future. The experience I have gained in knowledge management allows me to approach the task of family documentation with the highest professional standards.
    The combination of my professional experience and the visual digital tools we employ allows us to offer you a moving experience and personalized family documentation: from heritage films and roots books, to lightweight and accessible technological solutions that immortalize family memory.
    At Family PastPort, I bring all the knowledge I have accumulated to ensure that your story is documented and told, so that future generations will know where they came from and carry your name with pride. 
    🏛️Former Head of the Archives Division - Yad Vashem           📚Expert in Knowledge Management and Archival Research

    Our Mission

    At FAMILYPASTPORT, we believe that every family is a collection of precious stories, defining moments, and memories that must be preserved. Our mission is to make the family documentation process accessible, dignified, and meaningful – so that every generation can pass on its heritage with ease and confidence. 
    We believe that every family carries a unique story – a story of love, perseverance, immigration, renewal, and dreams. We document your family story with sensitivity and professionalism, transforming living memories into a written and visual legacy that will be passed down from generation to generation.
    Through personal interviews, in-depth archival research, and artistic editing, we build a living bridge between your family's past, present, and future.

    We accompany families with attentive care  and help them document the significant milestones in their lives – from photo albums to documents – before time passes. We consider it a privilege to be partners in your journey, and to provide you with peace of mind and the certainty that everything is properly documented.

    Through six dedicated services, we provide a comprehensive suite of family documentation solutions, combining professionalism, reliability, and human warmth.

    Our Process

    Accompanying you step by step on the path to perfect family documentation

    1

    Introductory Conversation

    We will get to know you and your family story. We will understand your needs, dreams, and wishes for documentation. Together we will build an initial vision for a project that reflects your family heritage.

    2

    Guided Story Collection

    We will embark on a shared journey of collecting memories, photos, and documents. We will guide you through a structured and sensitive process, and help you uncover the stories behind the pictures — the moments that made you a family.

    3

    Preparation of an Organized Keepsake

    We will process, design, and assemble a polished and impressive family documentation for you. The final product will be organized, accessible, and personalized — a keepsake that will be passed from generation to generation, one that every family member can connect with.
